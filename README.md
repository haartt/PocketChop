# PocketChop

**Turn any track into an instrument.** Chop, play, sequence and record — a pocket sampler for iPhone and iPad.

This repository hosts the PocketChop website: app info, support contact, and the privacy policy (see [`index.html`](index.html)).

## Features

- **Waveform chopping** — drag to slice, pinch to zoom, up to 16 chops per sample
- **16 pads** with per-pad attack, release, low-pass filter, level, delay, reverb and pitch
- **Pattern sequencer** with count-in, up to 8 patterns per session, adjustable loop length and BPM
- **Record** your full mix to M4A, or capture raw audio with the field recorder
- **Sessions** — save everything and pick up where you left off
- No account. No subscription.

## Privacy Policy

> **PocketChop collects no data. None.**

- **Audio stays on your device.** Songs you import, recordings you make with the microphone, and everything you create in the app are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad and are never uploaded, transmitted or shared with anyone.
- **Storage access** is required only to import audio files from your device, and to save your sketches and demos.
- **Microphone access** is requested only when you use the field recorder, and the audio it captures is saved only to your device.
- **No tracking.** PocketChop contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no account system. It makes no network requests.
- **Sharing is under your control.** If you export a chop or recording, it goes only where you send it via the iOS share sheet.

If this policy ever changes, the update will be published here. *Last updated: July 10, 2026.*
